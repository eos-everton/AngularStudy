Gelion Forest

In the north of Averion, amidst the frozen expanses of Iverskael, lies the imposing Gelion Forest. A region where the snow never melts and where the cutting winds whisper ancient secrets among the silver-branched trees. It is said that this forest is not only a refuge for wild animals but also home to ancestral guardians, mystical entities that watch over the balance of nature and protect the secrets hidden within.

Travelers who dare to enter Gelion know that there is no safe path. Trails form and disappear with the snowstorms, and the shadows of the trees seem to move at will. It is said that under the full moonlight, one can see slender figures walking among the trees, their eyes shining like distant stars.

Centuries ago, an ancient people inhabited the borders of Gelion. They were known as the Vëlarim, guardians of the forest and keepers of the knowledge of the frozen runes. Their leader, Eryon, had such a deep connection with the land that he could predict storms and understand the whispers of the winds. However, the Vëlarim disappeared long ago, leaving only their legends and relics buried beneath the snow.

One of the most told stories is that of the Silver Queen, an entity many believe to be the spirit of an ancient guardian of the Vëlarim. According to bards, she appears on nights of harsh winter, wrapped in a cloak made of snow and light. Those who cross her path say they feel a welcoming presence, but also an overwhelming force. Her duty is to protect the secrets of Gelion and prevent intruders from desecrating what lies hidden in the depths of the forest.

Rumors about what lies at the heart of Gelion vary. Some say there is a crystallized city where time has stopped and the spirits of the ancients still wander. Others believe that a powerful artifact rests beneath the roots of a colossal tree, capable of granting dominion over eternal winter. Many adventurers have tried to find such wonders, but few have returned to tell the tale.

Among those who returned, one name stands out: Kaelen, a relic hunter who claims to have seen the Silver Queen and walked through forgotten ruins. He carries with him a fragment of blue-icy crystal that glows under the moonlight and emits an almost imperceptible sound, like a distant whisper calling for a return to the forest. Kaelen knows that his journey is not yet over and that the secrets of Gelion still await those brave enough to uncover them.
